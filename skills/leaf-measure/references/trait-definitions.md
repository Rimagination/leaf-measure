# Trait Definitions

- `Area`: projected leaf area from the binary segmentation.
- `Perim.`: leaf perimeter from the segmented boundary.
- `Length`: Fiji Feret diameter, used here as leaf length.
- `Width `: Fiji minimum Feret diameter, used here as leaf width.
- `Circ.`: Fiji circularity metric derived from area and perimeter.
- `Solidity`: Fiji solidity metric, area divided by convex area.

Default outputs are in pixels.
